About

Let us introduce ourselves.

We are a locally owned DJ service that offers our services to any type of party or event in the northern Ohio Area.

Our Services are owned and operated out of Van Buren, OH. We stand by having affordable prices and cater to your needs for your event. The music at an event not only sets the mood for the guests and attendees, but it can make or break the moment and feel of the night. We aim to make sure that you're moment, is very memorable.
